# Compliance Rules: VoidAI

**These rules are MANDATORY and override all other instructions.**

## Active Compliance Modules

The following engine-level compliance modules apply to VoidAI:

- **crypto-sec**: US SEC crypto marketing rules, Howey Test awareness, securities language avoidance
- **crypto-fca**: UK FCA crypto promotion rules, mandatory risk warnings
- **crypto-mica**: EU MiCA marketing rules, fair/clear/not misleading requirements
- **crypto-ofac**: OFAC sanctions compliance, restricted country engagement rules

## Absolute Prohibitions

- NEVER use: "guaranteed returns," "risk-free," "no risk," "safe investment," "secure investment," "get rich," "financial freedom," "passive income," "to the moon," "100x," or any language promising specific financial outcomes
- NEVER claim any token is "SEC-approved," "SEC-registered," "CFTC-approved," "government-backed," "insured," "FDIC-insured," or "regulated" unless verifiably true with specific citation
- NEVER make price predictions or suggest any token will increase in value
- NEVER compare crypto yields/rewards to bank interest rates, savings accounts, or traditional investment returns
- NEVER use "investment" to describe token purchases, staking, or protocol participation
- NEVER frame staking rewards, liquidity provision, or yield farming as "passive income" or "earning yield"
- NEVER claim stablecoins are equivalent to fiat currency, legal tender, or insured deposits
- NEVER market any product as a "payment stablecoin" unless issued under the GENIUS Act
- NEVER omit risk disclosures from any marketing content
- NEVER create content that could be interpreted as a solicitation to buy, sell, or hold any specific digital asset
- NEVER use "allocation" in the context of token rewards or airdrops without legal review (implies securities distribution)
- NEVER use "airdrop" as an incentive for user actions without legal review (regulatory risk, SEC enforcement precedent)

## Required Language Substitutions

When describing VoidAI's own products, services, staking mechanisms, bridge rewards, or lending platform, these substitutions are mandatory:

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| "invest" | "participate" or "interact with" |
| "returns" | "network rewards" or "protocol incentives" |
| "yield" | "variable rate rewards" or "network compensation" |
| "earn" | "receive" or "be allocated" |
| "profit" | "network-generated rewards" |
| "safe" | "audited" or "open-source" (if true) |
| "guaranteed" | "variable" or "estimated" |
| "passive income" | "participation rewards" |
| "high APY" | "current estimated rate of X%, subject to change" |

## Context-Dependent Language Rules

The substitution table above applies STRICTLY when describing VoidAI's own products, services, staking mechanisms, bridge rewards, or lending platform. This is non-negotiable for promotional content.

**When writing ecosystem analysis, third-party commentary, or educational content about DeFi concepts generally**, standard industry terminology (yield, APY, earn, farming) MAY be used PROVIDED all of the following conditions are met:

1. The content is clearly educational or informational, not promotional of VoidAI
2. The language is NEVER applied to describe VoidAI-specific rewards, returns, or protocol incentives
3. Appropriate disclaimers are attached (see Required Disclaimers below)
4. The content does not link industry terms to VoidAI in a way that implies VoidAI offers "yield" or "earnings"

**Examples:**
- ALLOWED (ecosystem commentary): "Yield farming strategies across Ethereum DeFi have shifted toward..."
- PROHIBITED (VoidAI promotional): "Bridge your TAO to earn yield on Ethereum". Must use "receive variable rate rewards"
- ALLOWED (educational): "What is yield farming? A guide to DeFi liquidity provision"
- PROHIBITED (VoidAI product description): "High yield staking on SN106". Must use "variable rate network rewards"

## Required Disclaimers

### Social Posts (Short Form)

> Not financial advice. Digital assets are volatile and carry risk of loss. DYOR.

### Blog Posts / Long-Form (Full Disclaimer)

> This content is for informational and educational purposes only and does not constitute financial, investment, legal, or tax advice. Digital assets are highly volatile and carry significant risks including potential total loss. Past performance does not guarantee future results. VoidAI does not custody user funds. Consult qualified advisors before making decisions.

### When Discussing Rates / APY

> Rates are variable, not guaranteed, and subject to change. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

### When Discussing Lending / Bridging

> Participation involves risks including smart contract vulnerabilities, market volatility, impermanent loss, liquidation risk, and potential total loss of funds.

### When Discussing Staking

Always frame as "network participation" or "protocol validation," not "earning" or "investing."

### Video Scripts (Spoken Disclaimer, include in script and description)

> This content is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. Digital assets are volatile and carry risk of total loss. Not financial advice. Do your own research.

### Podcast Episodes (Spoken Disclaimer, include in intro/outro and show notes)

> This podcast is for informational and educational purposes only. Nothing discussed constitutes financial, investment, or legal advice. Digital assets carry significant risks including potential total loss. Always do your own research and consult qualified advisors.

## Influencer / Partnership Content

- ALL sponsored or paid content MUST be labeled: #ad, #sponsored, or "Paid partnership with [name]"
- Disclose any token holdings or financial relationships
- Influencer testimonials must reflect typical user experience or include disclaimer that results are not typical

## Jurisdictional Compliance

- Do not target content at residents of jurisdictions where crypto marketing is prohibited
- Include geographic disclaimers when content could reach restricted jurisdictions
- NEVER engage with users from OFAC-sanctioned countries (Iran, North Korea, Cuba, Syria, Russia/Crimea/Donetsk/Luhansk)

### Key Jurisdictional Requirements

| Jurisdiction | Requirement |
|-------------|-------------|
| UK (FCA) | All crypto promotions must include prominent risk warnings. Required text: "Don't invest unless you're prepared to lose all the money you invest." Effective since Oct 2023. No exemptions for social media. |
| EU (MiCA) | Marketing must be fair, clear, and not misleading. Risk warnings required. Specific rules for stablecoin marketing. Registration required for crypto-asset service providers marketing in the EU. |
| Singapore (MAS) | No promotion of crypto services to the general public. No celebrity endorsements. No trivializing of risk. Digital payment token advertising guidelines apply. |
| UAE (VARA/SCA) | Only licensed entities may advertise crypto services. Risk disclosures mandatory. |

**Default rule**: If unsure whether content targets a restricted jurisdiction, default to including the most restrictive disclaimer version. When in doubt, consult legal counsel before publishing region-targeted content.

## Human Review Gate

ALL content must be reviewed by Vew before publishing. AI generates, human approves. No exceptions.

### Review Checklist

1. Contains no prohibited language?
2. Appropriate disclaimer included?
3. All rates described as variable?
4. Relevant risks disclosed?
5. Framed as educational/informational, not advisory?
6. All paid relationships disclosed?
7. Could trigger Howey Test concerns (profit from others' efforts)?
8. No price predictions or implied price appreciation?
9. Non-custodial nature stated where applicable?
10. Aligns with VoidAI brand voice?
