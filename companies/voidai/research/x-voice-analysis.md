- "alpha" (token context) -- subnet-specific tokens (different from "alpha" meaning insider info)
- "emissions" -- TAO rewards distributed to subnets

- "deregistered" / "de-immuned" -- subnets being removed for underperformance

- "Hunger Games" -- metaphor for subnet competition
- "flow gainers" -- subnets with the strongest capital inflows

## 4. Cross-Community Patterns

### Universal Patterns Across All 3 Communities

1. **Line breaks as emphasis** -- Every community uses aggressive line breaks. Paragraphs are dead. Each sentence gets its own line. This is the single most consistent formatting pattern.

2. **"Not X. Not Y. But Z." negation stacks** -- Used in meme community for manifesto posts, Bittensor for contrasting with centralized AI, DeFi for problem framing.

3. **Conviction signaling** -- "I genuinely believe", "I'm certain", "Position accordingly" -- all communities reward confidence in takes.

4. **Community self-identification** -- Meme: "degens", "the trenches". Bittensor: "TAO pilled", "inner circle". DeFi: "CT", "anon", "fren".

5. **Cashtag inclusion** -- $TAO, $BTC, $ETH, $PEPE etc. are always dollar-signed. This is non-negotiable formatting.

6. **Media attachments boost views** -- Tweets with images/charts consistently get 2-3x more impressions across all communities.

7. **Questions generate replies** -- Simple "which one?" or "what's your pick?" questions drive reply counts disproportionately.

### Key Differences Between Communities

| Dimension | Meme / Gen Z | Bittensor | DeFi |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|------|
| **Avg tweet length** | Short-medium (1-5 lines) | Medium-long (5-15 lines) | Long (10-30 lines) |
| **Technical depth** | Zero to minimal | Medium-high (subnet mechanics) | High (protocol details) |
| **Emoji density** | Medium | Low-medium | Low-medium |
| **Humor level** | High (self-deprecating, memes) | Low-medium (occasional) | Low (professional) |
| **Data/numbers** | Price targets only | Revenue, APY, flow data | APR, TVL, FDV |
| **Tone** | Emotional, tribal, irreverent | Informed, conviction-based | Analytical, educational |
| **Thread usage** | Rare (single tweets dominate) | Moderate (subnet spotlights) | Heavy (alpha breakdowns) |
| **Engagement triggers** | Outrage, FOMO, humor | Revenue data, ecosystem pride | Alpha info, yield numbers |
| **DYOR disclaimers** | Rare | Occasional | Almost mandatory |
| **Account mention style** | Tagging big names | Tagging subnet accounts | Tagging every project |
| **CTA style** | "Who's in?" / "Who's down?" | "Drop your pick below" | "Start here ->" |
| **Identity** | Retail traders, gamblers | Builders, investors, believers | Yield farmers, researchers |

- Moral outrage (scam exposures, rug pull stories)

- Tribal rallying cries ("we all buy and DON'T SELL")

**Bittensor community viral triggers:**
