## Recommendations

### Immediate (Week 1)

- **Audit and update the page**: Ensure logo, banner, about section, and website link are current
- **Encourage team members to link profiles**: Every team member should list VoidAI as their employer
- **Cross-post the Void 2.0 announcement**: Repurpose the X launch post as a LinkedIn article or update
- **Add a featured section**: Link to the website, blog post, and any press coverage

### Short-Term (Month 1)

- **Establish a posting cadence**: 2–3 posts/week, repurposed from X content but adapted for LinkedIn's professional tone
- **Post milestone updates**: Bridge volume milestones, partnership announcements, product launches
- **Share team content**: "Meet the team" posts, engineering blog excerpts, behind-the-scenes
- **Publish a long-form article**: "What is Cross-Chain Interoperability for Bittensor?" — educational content performs well on LinkedIn

### Medium-Term (Month 2–3)

- **Automate cross-posting**: Use Outstand API or n8n to automatically adapt and post X content to LinkedIn
- **Build a follower base**: Engage with Bittensor, Chainlink, and DeFi community posts on LinkedIn
- **Post job listings**: Even exploratory "expressions of interest" posts attract attention
- **Track analytics**: Monitor follower growth, post impressions, and engagement rate monthly
