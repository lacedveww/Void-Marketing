## What Happened

Templar (@tplr_ai) completed the largest decentralized LLM training run in history:

- **Model:** Covenant-72B (72 billion parameters)
- **Data:** Approximately 1.1 trillion tokens
- **Infrastructure:** Bittensor Subnet 3 (SN3), no centralized compute cluster
- **Method:** Fully decentralized training across distributed GPU contributors on the Bittensor network

This is a landmark event for decentralized AI. Previous decentralized training runs were limited to much smaller models (typically under 10B parameters). Training a 72B model without a centralized cluster demonstrates that Bittensor's incentive mechanism and subnet architecture can coordinate the compute resources needed for frontier-scale model training.
