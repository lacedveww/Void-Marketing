### Significant Changes to Flag
1. **TAO price surged**: From ~$221 (Mar 13 baseline) to ~$265-289 (Mar 15). A 64.7% weekly gain driven by AI token rotation and Bittensor's Covenant-72B milestone announcement on Mar 15. This is a volatile moment; content referencing TAO price should use ranges or note the rally.
2. **TAO 24h volume doubled**: From $231M to $468M, reflecting the surge.
3. **VoidAI 2.0 is live**: Launched Feb 10, 2026. Multi-chain via Chainlink CCIP. This is a major milestone that should be reflected in all forward-looking content.
4. **Project Rubicon is live**: Competitor launched Nov 2025 with 17 subnets on Base. Active on Aerodrome. Content should acknowledge this competitive landscape shift.
5. **Subnet cap expansion to 256**: Planned for 2026. Content currently says "128" or "128+" which is still accurate, but the planned expansion to 256 is a notable narrative for growth content.

### Mindshare Data Reliability Warning
The Altcoin Buzz "Top 10" article with the 2.01% / #5 ranking is the only source found. The article URL (altcoinbuzz.io/cryptocurrency-news/top-10-bittensor-subnets-by-mindshare/) does not include a date in the URL path, and the data was also cited in the Sep 2025 metrics-baseline. **There is a meaningful risk this is the same stale data.** The current top 4 (Ridges 2.43%, Chutes 2.36%, Bitcast 2.26%, Dippy 2.07%) could have shifted. To get truly fresh mindshare data, check kaito.ai/mindshare directly or use Kaito's API.

However, the team should be aware of it and may want to investigate what's driving the low Security (25/100) and Compliance (30/100) scores, as these could surface in due diligence by potential partners or investors.
