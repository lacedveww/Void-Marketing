## What Is Happening

Two trends are converging in AI hardware:

### Trend 1: On-Device LLMs Getting Viable

Qwen3.5 35B is running on an iPhone at 5.6 tokens/second. A 35-billion parameter model on a phone. This was considered impossible 18 months ago. The key enablers:
- Quantization improvements (4-bit and lower with minimal quality loss)
- Apple's Neural Engine and M-series chip efficiency
- Model architecture improvements (Mixture of Experts, attention optimizations)
- Framework improvements (llama.cpp, MLX)

### Trend 2: Dedicated Local AI Hardware

Perplexity is selling a "Personal Computer" based on a Mac mini that runs AI 24/7 locally. This signals a shift from cloud-first to local-first AI inference. Key implication: if consumers run AI locally, they don't need to pay OpenAI/Google/Anthropic for API calls.
