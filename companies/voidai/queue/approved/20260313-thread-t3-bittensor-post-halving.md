## Thread Parts

### Part 1

Bittensor's first halving hit December 15, 2025. Emissions dropped from 7,200 to 3,600 $TAO per day. Three months later, here's what actually happened to the network.

Not financial advice. DYOR.

### Part 2

The basics: Bittensor's halving is supply-based, not time-based. Triggered when supply crossed 10.5M $TAO. Inflation: ~26% to ~13%.

Next halving: ~2029, at 15.75M TAO. Max supply: 21M, same cap as Bitcoin.

### Part 3

Pre-halving, 7,200 TAO/day flowed across all subnets. That was the growth fuel, subsidizing miners, validators, and subnet operators to bootstrap the network.

Post-halving, every subnet competes for a smaller pool. 3,600 TAO/day. Same 128+ subnets. The math changed.

### Part 4

Network response: 68% of $TAO is now staked. Holders locking supply, not selling into reduced emissions.

$TAO holding a top-40 CMC rank with a multi-billion dollar market cap. Market absorbed the halving without structural disruption. Check current price on CoinGecko or CoinMarketCap.

### Part 5

Subnet economics shifted. Emissions halved = real pressure to demonstrate value. dTAO makes it explicit. Holders direct incentives toward performing subnets.

128+ subnets at capacity. 4-month immunity for new ones. After that, produce or get replaced.

### Part 6

Pre-halving growth (Q2 2025): +50% subnets, +16% miners, +28% non-zero wallets, +21.5% staked TAO.

The question now: do reduced emissions slow adoption or concentrate activity in higher-quality subnets?

### Part 7

AI narrative holding: $TAO is the 2nd most-mentioned AI coin on LunarCrush. Mindshare driven by subnet growth and dev activity. Grayscale filed for a Bittensor Trust in Dec 2025.

Institutional interest arriving as emissions halved.

### Part 8

Where VoidAI fits: SN106 operates in this post-halving environment. Reduced emissions mean every subnet must deliver measurable value to attract staked $TAO under dTAO.

Top-5 Kaito mindshare among Bittensor subnets as of September 2025. The competition for emissions share is real.

### Part 9

The halving was Bittensor's first economic stress test. Reduced issuance, sustained staking, subnet competition intensifying.

Full analysis + what it means for subnet operators: voidai.com
