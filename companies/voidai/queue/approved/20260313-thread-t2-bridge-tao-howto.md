### Part 2

Step 1: Go to app.voidai.com/bridge-chains. Connect your Bittensor wallet (Polkadot.js or compatible) and your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc).

No account creation. No KYC. Non-custodial. You control both sides.

### Part 3

Step 2: Select source (Bittensor) and destination (Solana). Enter TAO amount.

Lock-and-mint: your TAO locks in a non-custodial contract. @chainlink CCIP verifies the lock cross-chain. No intermediary.

### Part 4

Step 3: Confirm. CCIP transmits the verified message to Solana. wTAO mints 1:1 to your Solana wallet.

Your funds stay in smart contracts the entire time. VoidAI never takes custody.

### Part 5

Step 4: Your wTAO is now on Solana. Swap on @RaydiumProtocol or Jupiter. Provide liquidity. Use it across Solana DeFi protocols.

Going back? Same process in reverse. Burn wTAO on Solana, unlock TAO on Bittensor.

### Part 6

Why CCIP? Bridges have lost billions to exploits. Chainlink's CCIP is used across major DeFi protocols. Established validation, not custom trust assumptions.
