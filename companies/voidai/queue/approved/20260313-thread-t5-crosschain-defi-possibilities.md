wTAO is a standard SPL token. It plugs into the full Solana DeFi stack.

LP provision: wTAO pairs live on @RaydiumProtocol CLMM. Concentrated liquidity for wTAO/SOL and wTAO/USDC. SN106 miners already provide liquidity here.

Swaps and routing: wTAO is routable through Jupiter aggregator. That means access to best-price execution across Solana DEXs. No manual DEX hopping.

TAO holders get the same swap infrastructure Solana-native tokens use.
