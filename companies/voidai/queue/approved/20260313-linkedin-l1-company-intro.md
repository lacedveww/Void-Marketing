## Content

**VoidAI: Connecting Bittensor's Intelligence to Global Liquidity**

Bittensor runs 128+ active subnets producing real computational output: inference, training, storage, code generation. Grayscale filed for a Bittensor Trust in December 2025. Non-zero wallet count grew 28% in a single quarter.

But TAO and subnet alpha tokens have limited access to broader DeFi ecosystems. Holders face multi-step processes through centralized intermediaries, adding cost, complexity, and custodial risk.

VoidAI was built to close that gap.

**What VoidAI Does**

Cross-chain infrastructure purpose-built for Bittensor:

- Non-custodial bridge to Solana and EVM chains, secured by Chainlink CCIP
- SN106, a Bittensor subnet coordinating on-chain liquidity provisioning on Raydium CLMM
- Developer SDK for programmatic bridge and subnet access
- Lending protocol (in development) for liquidity against bridged assets

Multi-product infrastructure, not a single-function bridge.

**How It Works**

Lock-and-mint via Chainlink CCIP: TAO locks on Bittensor, CCIP verifies the message, wrapped tokens mint on the destination chain. Reverse to redeem. Open source: github.com/v0idai.

**Ecosystem Context**

Project Rubicon serves the Base/Coinbase pathway. LayerZero's vTAO bridges to EVM chains. VoidAI bridges to Solana and EVM, with native Raydium DEX integration and concentrated liquidity coordination via SN106. Different target ecosystems, complementary approaches.

VoidAI ranked top-5 in Bittensor Kaito mindshare as of September 2025, the only liquidity-focused subnet in the top 10.

**What's Next**

- Lending platform launch
- Additional chain support
- SN106 scoring and emission improvements
- Continued open source development
