## Thread Parts

### Part 1
Subnet Spotlight: Chutes (SN64)

Serverless GPU inference on Bittensor. No containers, no cold starts. You send a request, Chutes routes it to available compute.

### Part 2
The problem Chutes solves: running inference at scale is expensive and slow to provision. Cloud GPU providers require container setup, minimum commitments, cold boot times.

Chutes abstracts all of that. Push a model, get an endpoint. Miners compete on speed and cost.

### Part 3
How it works: model providers deploy to the Chutes network. Miners bid compute resources. The subnet's validation layer verifies inference quality and latency.

Bad outputs get penalized. Fast, accurate miners get more traffic. Market-driven quality.

### Part 4
What makes SN64 different from other inference subnets: the serverless abstraction layer.

SN19 and SN4 focus on raw inference verification. Chutes focuses on the developer experience of deploying and calling models without managing infrastructure.

### Part 5
Worth watching if you track Bittensor compute economics. Serverless is where cloud is heading. SN64 is building that pattern on decentralized hardware.
