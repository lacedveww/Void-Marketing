# Crisis Communication Protocol

## When to Activate

Activate this protocol immediately if ANY of the following occur:

{{CRISIS_TRIGGERS}}

Examples:
- Service disruption affecting users
- Security vulnerability or exploit
- Community backlash or coordinated attack
- Regulatory action or legal threat
- Competitor disinformation campaign
- Leaked internal documents

## Immediate Response (First 30 Minutes)

1. **PAUSE all scheduled content** across ALL accounts immediately
2. **Do NOT delete or edit** any prior posts about security, safety, or performance
3. **Do NOT engage** with speculation, FUD, or trolls
4. **Assess scope**: What happened? What is affected? What do we know vs. don't know?
5. **Draft factual acknowledgment** (human-approved ONLY)

## Response Templates

### Technical Incident
> We are aware of [specific issue]. [Impact assessment]. Our team is [specific action]. We will update every [timeframe]. Thread below with details.

### Community/Reputational Incident
> We've seen the discussion about [topic]. Here's the context: [factual explanation]. We take this seriously and [specific action]. Happy to answer questions directly.

### Regulatory/Legal
> We are aware of [development]. We are reviewing with counsel and will share updates as appropriate. {{COMPANY_NAME}} remains committed to [compliance/transparency/user protection].

## Per-Account Crisis Behavior

| Account | During Crisis |
|---------|--------------|
| Main | ONLY account that posts official updates. Factual, transparent, no spin. |
| {{SATELLITE_ACCOUNTS}} | Configure per account: SILENT, or may share official updates via QT only. |

## What NEVER To Do During a Crisis

- Continue posting promotional content or engagement bait
- Delete or edit prior posts about security, safety, or performance
- Make unverified claims about user safety
- Promise compensation before assessment is complete
- Go silent for more than 4 hours during an active incident
- Blame users or external parties without verified evidence
- Minimize the situation

## Post-Crisis

- Publish post-mortem within 72 hours
- Update brand/voice-learnings.md with crisis communication lessons
- Resume satellite activity gradually (most serious accounts first, culture/meme last)
- Review and update this protocol
