```
8AM/8PM ET: Intelligence Sweep (SILENT)
    |-- Reads: monitoring/content-accounts.md (Tier 1)
    |-- Reads: monitoring/marketing-accounts.md (Tier 2)
    |-- Runs:  collect-news.sh, collect-metrics.sh
    |
    v
data/sweep-YYYY-MM-DD-HHMM.json
    |
    v
9AM ET: Morning Summary -> Discord/Telegram brief
    |
    v
10:30AM ET: Draft Review + Scheduler
    |-- Generates 3-5 drafts (Pillar A: 2-3, Pillar B: 1-2)
    |-- Human approval gate
    |-- Schedules via OpenTweet
    |
    v
10PM ET: Engagement Collector (SILENT)
    |-- Updates performance-summary.json
    |-- Feeds back into next morning's generation
    |
    v
Fri 12PM ET: Weekly Recap + Voice Calibration
    |-- Recap thread posted to @v0idai
    |-- voice-learnings.md updated
    |-- Calibration triggers checked
```
